The Builder

Edwin Wood

I’ve always gravitated toward systems — not just how they’re built, but how they connect people, solve problems, and evolve over time. Whether leading a customer platform redesign or modernizing internal tools, I find energy in shaping technology that actually moves the business forward. I build with intention, always aiming for clarity, utility, and momentum.

The Leader

Edwin Wood

Leadership, for me, is about showing up consistently — especially when things get hard. I’ve led teams through mergers, re-orgs, and high-growth phases, always keeping people at the center. I’m proud of the trust I’ve built across departments and the way I’ve helped others grow into their own leadership. Titles come and go, but how you lead sticks.

The Strategist

Edwin Wood

I’ve been fortunate to operate at the intersection of business and technology — seeing how tools and processes can either slow a team down or unlock something new. I like asking hard questions, pushing for better alignment, and translating complexity into something actionable. I think in terms of systems, incentives, and long-term value.

The Survivor

Edwin Wood

Life doesn’t always follow a straight path. After surviving rectal cancer at 42, I returned to school to finish what I started over a decade ago. That experience reshaped my perspective — on urgency, on purpose, and on why I care so deeply about the work I do. I’m still building, still learning, and more motivated than ever.
